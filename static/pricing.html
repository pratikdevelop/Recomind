FAQ

Can I pay in Indian Rupees? ▼
Yes. We use PayPal which supports INR payments via credit card, debit card, UPI-linked PayPal accounts, and net banking.
Can I cancel anytime? ▼
Absolutely. Cancel from your dashboard at any time. Your plan stays active until the end of the billing period, then reverts to Free.
What happens to my documents if I downgrade? ▼
Your documents are kept safely in the database. You just won't be able to upload new ones until you're within the Free plan limits.
Do you offer a free trial for Pro? ▼
The Free plan is your trial — use it as long as you like. When you're ready for more queries and documents, upgrade to Pro with one click.
Is my data safe? ▼
Yes. Each user's documents are fully isolated in MongoDB. Your data is never used to train any model and is never shared with other users.
